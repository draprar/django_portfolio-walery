Who I am

Hey there! I’m Walery — an engineer, programmer and analyst with over a decade of experience in production, electrical and telecom fields, plus a few years of practice in Python, data and automation. Day to day I handle technical stuff: from Python and ML, through data flows and automations, reports, audits and design, all the way to custom-built tools.

I bring three perspectives to the table: analytical — turning numbers into value, engineering — with a structured approach to problems, and humanistic — keeping the message clear and simple.

After hours I draw, paint and build my own web projects — like an art gallery or a tongue-twister platform. This site is both my portfolio and part of a modular Django platform I’m developing. Below are the areas I move in — all tied together by one thing: I like building stuff that works.

What I Can Help With

Telecom & Ops

Who I am

Hi! I’m Walery — an engineer, programmer and analyst with over a decade of experience in production, electrical and telecom industries, and several years of practice in Python, data analysis, and automation. On a daily basis, I work on documentation, design, reporting, audits, machine learning, and custom-built tools — from automation to data-driven solutions.

I combine three perspectives: analytical — turning numbers into value, engineering — with a structured approach to problems, and humanistic — emphasizing clarity and practical usability.

After hours I draw, paint and develop my own web projects — like an online gallery or a tongue-twister platform. Below are the areas I operate in and where I am happy to help — all tied together by one thing: I enjoy building things that work. If you’re looking for someone who combines engineering with creativity — get in touch! I’d be glad to take on a new challenge.

PS. Even if your idea doesn’t fit the listed areas, write to me — we’ll figure something out.

What I Can Help With

Telecommunication & Electrical

Years of experience in the telecom, electrical and industrial (R&D) sectors — from design and analysis, through cost optimization and risk management, to audits and quality control. I bring expertise from environments where there’s no room for technical or organizational compromise.

Data Analysis, Automation & ML

I design and implement data-driven solutions — from pipelines and process automation in Python to predictive models and Jupyter analyses. I combine engineering insight with data science to create tools that deliver real business value.

Technical Documentation & Standards

I create and manage technical documentation — projects, guidelines, engineering rules and in-house standards. I ensure clarity, compliance and real usability, so even complex topics are easy to understand and ready for implementation.

Training & Mentoring

I deliver technical training and mentoring, create educational materials and build development programs. I combine expert knowledge with instructor experience to help people and teams grow in skills and efficiency.

Program, Project & Team Management

Planning, scheduling, tender coordination and leading project teams — both in office and on site. My background in infrastructure projects helps me bridge technical, business and organizational perspectives into one coherent approach.

Custom Solutions

I build applications, automations and tools — from MVPs to full-scale systems. I integrate data, develop predictive models and optimize workflows, delivering reliable code and working solutions without unnecessary extras.

Visualization & Reporting

I design reports and dashboards in Power BI, Looker and Excel that connect data with narrative. My visualizations highlight key metrics, support decisions and drive efficiency — no decorative charts, only insights that matter.

Creativity & Applied Art

I draw, paint and design — from graphics and murals to custom-painted fitness equipment. I translate aesthetics into practice: branding, visuals and unique promotional materials that stand out through quality and originality.

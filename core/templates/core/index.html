Who I am

Hi! I’m Walery — an engineer, programmer and analyst. I have over a decade of experience in the electrical and telecom industries and several years’ practice in Python, data analysis and modeling.

On a daily basis I work on analytics, technical documentation and audits, but what I enjoy most is designing and building tools that genuinely improve workflows — from automation and data pipelines to reporting and machine-learning models.

I most often work with Python (pandas, scikit-learn), SQL and Power BI. I also use Django and FastAPI in projects, and tie everything together with Git — mainly for building apps, automation and analytical tools.

After hours I draw, paint and develop my own web projects — realizing my own ideas and testing new solutions.

If you need someone who can translate an idea into a working tool — get in touch.

What I Can Help With

Data, Automation & Development

I design and implement data-driven solutions — from Python pipelines and automation to ML models and web applications. I combine engineering experience with analytics to build tools that streamline processes and support decisions.

Standards, Documentation & Training

I create technical documentation, engineering guidelines and standards. I also conduct audits and training, ensuring clarity, compliance and practical knowledge transfer within teams.

Visualization, Reporting & Creativity

I design reports and dashboards in Power BI and Excel that present data clearly and support decision-making. After hours I draw and paint, combining aesthetics with a functional approach to design.
